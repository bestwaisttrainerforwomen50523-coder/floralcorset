Tips Before Buying a Floral Corset

When choosing the right floral corset, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Check the boning type: Steel boned corsets offer more support, while plastic boned corsets are lighter and more flexible.
  • Pick breathable fabrics: For daily wear, cotton or mesh-backed corsets are more comfortable than heavy satin.
  • Choose your size carefully: Corsets should fit snugly but not painfully. Always follow a brand’s size chart.
  • Consider the pattern: Small floral prints are subtle and elegant, while bold flowers create a striking fashion statement.
